Frequently Asked Questions

Discover how our product seamlessly integrates with various tools and platforms, ensuring compatibility for diverse workflows.

Is EXONOMA a fund?

No. It’s an operating platform with AI tools, reporting and support.

Do you guarantee returns?

No. We communicate estimated APY and risks. No guarantees.

Can I stop the plan whenever I want?

Yes, you can pause or change at any time, subject to cut-offs in the Terms.

What is dynamic hedging?

A system that adapts coverage levels to reduce drawdowns in negative phases.

Who holds the funds?

Regulated partners with segregation and traceability per policy. See “Compliance”.

How are fees calculated?

Subscription + any performance components per public policies. Fully disclosed.

What risks should I expect?

Market risk, counterparty risk, liquidity and execution risk. Read the Risk Disclosure.

What’s in the Pro plan?

Advanced hedging, real-time alerts, periodic calls, extended features.

Can SMEs use EXONOMA?

Yes. Multi-account, roles, accounting exports and reports.

Do you have audits?

We operate compliance-first and work with external partners. Audit status is shown in the dashboard when applicable.

How do you protect my data?

Encryption, 2FA, role-based access, log audits, backups and recovery plans.

What are you not?

Not personalized financial advice. Not a “black box.” We rely on method + transparency.

Still Have a Question?

Contact us directly if you have any doubt we are happy to help you!
